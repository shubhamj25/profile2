ABOUT ENTREPRENURSHIP

Entrepreneurship is the process of designing, launching and running a new business, which is often initially a small business. The people who create these businesses are called entrepreneurs. Entrepreneurship has been described as the "capacity and willingness to develop, organize and manage a business venture along with any of its risks in order to make a profit.

EDC ,Thapar university

Entrepreneurship Development Cell, Thapar University commonly known as EDC, has been established with the objective of creating, fostering and promoting the spirit of entrepreneurship among the youth of the nation. Created in the early months of 2014, the cell has already taken rapid strides in cultivating entrepreneurial temper and has succeeded in carving a niche for itself.

Our Aim

Our aim is to bridge the gap between the ideas and their implementation and give wings to the ideas nesting in the minds of the students.

Our Vision

Our vision is to develop entrepreneurial ecosystem of India by bringing the leading edge ideas to the forefront and converting them into business ideas.
